3. **Simulation evidence:** It supports the narrow teaching claims, not more. The reweighting sim is “rigged” only if overstated: measured drift is exactly a two-stratum mix shift with perfectly observed strata and a correct fixed reference mix, so reweighting must win by construction [rung1-recovery-sim.py](/Users/brettreynolds/Documents/LLM-CLI-projects/papers/Difference-in-Differences_for_Corpus_Linguistics/simulations/rung1-recovery-sim.py:68). That is fine as a sanity check, not as validation for real corpora. The `malign_unmeasured` arm is not a strawman; it is the missing premise made visible: no unmeasured differential within-stratum drift. But it should be described as nonidentifiability, not just “reweighting fails” [rung1-recovery-sim.py](/Users/brettreynolds/Documents/LLM-CLI-projects/papers/Difference-in-Differences_for_Corpus_Linguistics/simulations/rung1-recovery-sim.py:16). The ladder sim is too easy: the composition diagnostic is almost an oracle, the wire/interference variable is passed in as the true bleed parameter, and there is one seed rather than misclassification rates over adversarial parameter ranges [rung1-ladder-sim.py](/Users/brettreynolds/Documents/LLM-CLI-projects/papers/Difference-in-Differences_for_Corpus_Linguistics/simulations/rung1-ladder-sim.py:115).

4. **Still wrong or missing:**
- The inference claim missing its premise: diagnostics are treated as accurately observed. Real composition margins, AFP exposure, outlet identity, and referent detection will be noisy and incomplete.
- The threshold claim missing its premise: ladder cutoffs are DGP-tuned, not calibrated to external evidence or operating characteristics.
- The pooling claim missing its premise: current pooling demonstration is a mechanical null because shrinking within pre/post periods preserves the period means.

5. **Single most important fix:** Before drafting the rest, add an adversarial simulation grid: noisy measured margins, partial unmeasured drift, imperfect wire/exposure measurement, treatment-by-stratum heterogeneity, sparse/zero cells, and near-threshold cases. Report misrouting rates, not just five successful arms. Then rewrite the prose so the sims are “sanity checks plus failure maps,” not validation that the pipeline works.
