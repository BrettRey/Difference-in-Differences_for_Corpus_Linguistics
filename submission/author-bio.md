# Author Short Biography

Brett Reynolds is a linguist at Humber Polytechnic and the University of Toronto. His research concerns English grammar and syntactic categorization in the framework of the Cambridge Grammar of the English Language, lexicography, and the application of homeostatic property cluster (HPC) theory from the philosophy of science to grammatical questions, including category membership, countability, definiteness, and grammaticality.
